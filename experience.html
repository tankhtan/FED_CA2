personal projects

Grannehbot:
a personal discord bot

Grannehbot's personality was inspired by a character in my favorite game. I coded the bot using python and installed it on me and my friend's private discord server. It could respond to a wide range of commands, and remind us of important deadlines at specific timings.

An automated AFK clicker

A mobile game I enjoyed playing required the player to sit idle in the game for long periods of time to earn currency. Hence, I attached a stylus to a servomotor and programmed it to tap my phone screen every few minutes so that I could do other things while collecting the currency.

A contactless bus bell

I built and programmed a bus bell that could be activated just by the wave of a hand. It utilized an ultrasonic sensor to detect a hand in front of the bell, and a relay to ring the bell.
